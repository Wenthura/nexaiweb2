What is a Vedic Birth Chart?

A Vedic birth chart (Kundali or Janma Kundali) is a map of the sky at the exact moment you were born. Unlike Western astrology, Vedic astrology uses the sidereal zodiac — tracking where the planets actually are in the sky right now, not where they were 2,000 years ago.

Your birth chart contains 12 houses, each governing a different area of life — from personality and wealth to relationships and career. The positions of the 9 Vedic planets (Navagraha) across these houses create your unique cosmic blueprint.

How Grahachara Calculates Your Chart

Grahachara uses the Swiss Ephemeris — the same NASA-grade astronomical data used by observatories worldwide — to calculate planetary positions accurate to the arc-minute. We factor in:

  • Ayanamsa correction — precise sidereal offset (Lahiri)
  • All 9 Vedic planets — Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu
  • 27 Nakshatras — lunar mansions with their Padas
  • 12 Bhava houses — using your exact birth time and location
  • Planetary aspects (Drishti) — how planets influence each other
  • Vimshottari Dasha — planetary periods spanning your entire life

What Your Birth Chart Reveals

Every house in your chart tells a story:

  • 1st House (Lagna) — Your personality, physical appearance, and life approach
  • 7th House — Marriage, partnerships, and relationships
  • 10th House — Career, public reputation, and life purpose
  • 5th House — Creativity, children, and romance
  • 9th House — Fortune, spirituality, and higher learning
  • 2nd & 11th Houses — Wealth, income, and financial gains

Grahachara interprets every placement, aspect, and Dasha period to give you a complete understanding of your cosmic design — not generic paragraphs, but readings unique to your exact moment of birth.

Why Vedic Over Western?

Western astrology uses the tropical zodiac, which has drifted approximately 24° from actual star positions due to the precession of equinoxes. Vedic astrology corrects for this drift, meaning your planets are placed where they actually were in the sky when you were born. The result: more precise predictions and timing.
