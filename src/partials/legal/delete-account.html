How to Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Grahachara account and all associated data. Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Grahachara app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request via Email

Send an email to support@grahachara.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the Google email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information — Your name, email address, and Google profile data
  • Birth chart data — Date, time, and location of birth
  • Generated reports — All AI-generated horoscope and porondam reports
  • Chat history — All conversations with the AI astrology assistant
  • Subscription data — Your subscription status and purchase history (linked to your account)
  • Token balance — Any remaining report tokens

What Data May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal and business requirements:

  • Transaction records — Payment transaction IDs may be retained for up to 12 months for financial reporting and refund processing as required by law
  • Anonymized analytics — Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data

Important Notes

  • If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting account deletion. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription.
  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Grahachara again.
  • If you have any questions, contact us at support@grahachara.com
